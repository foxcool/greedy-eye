# Product Context - Greedy Eye

## Product Vision

Greedy Eye is a comprehensive **personal financial management system** that empowers individuals, families, and businesses to organize, track, and optimize their entire financial portfolio across all asset classes and financial instruments.

**Starting with cryptocurrency** as the primary use case, the system will evolve to encompass traditional banking, investment accounts, real estate, lending platforms, and personal financial instruments - creating a unified view of one's complete financial ecosystem.

## Target Users

### Primary Users

- **Individual Investors**: Managing personal portfolios across crypto, stocks, real estate, and other assets
- **Active Traders**: Seeking arbitrage opportunities and automated trading strategies
- **DeFi Enthusiasts**: Tracking on-chain assets and protocol interactions
- **Family Financial Managers**: Organizing household finances across multiple accounts and asset types
- **Small Business Owners**: Managing business finances, investments, and cash flow
- **Real Estate Investors**: Tracking property portfolios, rental income, and mortgage obligations

### User Personas

1. **Alex the Arbitrageur**: Professional trader focused on cross-exchange crypto opportunities
2. **Maria the Portfolio Manager**: Long-term investor optimizing asset allocation across all classes
3. **David the DeFi Farmer**: Yield farmer tracking multiple protocols and chains
4. **Elena the Family CFO**: Managing household finances, savings, and investments
5. **Sergey the Real Estate Investor**: Tracking rental properties, mortgages, and property income
6. **Anna the Small Business Owner**: Managing business accounts, investments, and cash flow

## Core Value Propositions

### For Individual Investors

- **Unified Financial View**: Aggregate holdings across crypto, banking, brokerage, real estate, and other assets
- **Real-time Analytics**: Portfolio performance, risk metrics, and asset allocation across all financial instruments
- **Automated Alerts**: Price movements, rebalancing opportunities, and market changes
- **Expense Tracking**: Monitor spending patterns and financial health across all accounts

### For Active Traders

- **Arbitrage Detection**: Real-time price spread monitoring across exchanges and asset classes
- **Automated Execution**: Strategy-based trading with risk management
- **Backtesting**: Historical analysis of trading strategies
- **Multi-asset Opportunities**: Cross-asset arbitrage and correlation analysis

### For DeFi Users

- **Multi-chain Tracking**: Ethereum, Arbitrum, Optimism, and other networks
- **Protocol Integration**: Uniswap, Curve, Compound, and other DeFi protocols
- **Yield Optimization**: Comparing returns across different farming opportunities
- **Cross-platform Yield**: Compare DeFi yields with traditional lending platforms

### For Family Financial Management

- **Household Portfolio**: Unified view of family finances across all members
- **Expense Categorization**: Track spending patterns and budget adherence
- **Savings Goals**: Monitor progress toward financial objectives
- **Risk Management**: Family-wide risk assessment and allocation

### For Real Estate Investors

- **Property Portfolio**: Track multiple properties, mortgages, and rental income
- **Cash Flow Analysis**: Monitor rental income vs. expenses and mortgage payments
- **Property Performance**: Compare real estate returns with other investments
- **Tax Optimization**: Track deductible expenses and tax implications

## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks

- **API Integration Complexity**: Maintain adapter pattern for diverse financial institutions
- **Rate Limiting**: Implement efficient caching and request optimization
- **Data Accuracy**: Cross-validate data from multiple sources
- **Security**: Enhanced security for sensitive financial data

### Market Risks

- **Regulatory Changes**: Design for compliance across multiple jurisdictions
- **Competition**: Focus on unique value propositions and comprehensive coverage
- **User Adoption**: Prioritize user experience and reliable functionality
- **Financial Institution Cooperation**: Build relationships with banks and platforms

### Operational Risks

- **Security**: Implement best practices for financial data management
- **Scalability**: Design for growth from the beginning
- **Reliability**: Ensure high uptime and data consistency
- **Compliance**: Maintain regulatory compliance across all features
